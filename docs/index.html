How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

Set up once, then sit back and watch your chat light up the stream.

Your viewers redeem

Chat uses Channel Points to request a GIF or play a sound — just like any other reward on your channel.

Visema queues it

Requests line up automatically. Nothing overlaps, and invalid ones get refunded — zero headaches.

It appears on stream

GIFs and sounds play right on your OBS overlay. Your audience sees it live, in real time.

Why Visema

Built for streamers, not engineers.

Everything you need to make your chat more interactive — nothing you don't.

Runs entirely on your machine

No cloud, no third-party servers. Your stream data never leaves your computer — fast and private.

Configurable to your taste

Adjust GIF size, queue limits, cooldown timers, volume — tweak everything through a simple config file.

GIFs and sounds in one queue

A single smart queue handles both media types. No overlapping, no chaos — just smooth playback.

Zero maintenance

Set it up once and forget about it. Visema stays connected to Twitch and OBS in the background.
